About

  • Lazar's Learning is a free, handpicked database for various textbooks on math topics.
  • I aim to make learning advanced math more accessible to anyone who wants to learn it.
  • We sort our information along a wide range of topics, with more added as we come across new good materals
  • All resources are accompanied with a short review that entails the pros and cons of the resource, along with who I believe would benefit from it. Remember: no resource is perfect!

FAQ

  • Is this a complete list?
  • No, and it does not try to be. This is only a selection of the best resources I have seen, and is ever expanding. I may even remove some entries if I find a resource that outclasses it in its own niche.
  • Is this list free?
  • Yes, the entire list is free to everyone
  • Are all resources free?
  • No. Unfortunately, most textbooks are quite expensive. Free ones will be noted, and out of respect for the authors, I will not say where to find the paid ones. No, this list contains no informations on websites such as Anna's archive, that promise free books. No such information at all
